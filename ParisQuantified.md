# Paris Quantified
## Setup of the Single Board Computer
I am using the [Raspberry Pi Zero W](https://www.raspberrypi.org/products/raspberry-pi-zero-w/). Important for me were integrated WiFi and low power consumption. I chose an "industrial" SD card, a 16GB Lexar High-Performance C10 microSDH (LFSDM10-16GABC10) hoping it will have a long life time even though it is installed outside where ambient temperature will fluctuate. I installed Raspberry Pi OS (32-bit) Lite.
